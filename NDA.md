## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

**"Confidential Information"** means all non-public information disclosed by the Company to the Recipient, whether orally, visually, in writing, or in electronic form, including but not limited to:

- Source code, object code, and compiled binaries
- Software architecture, system design, and technical documentation
- Database schemas, API specifications, and protocol designs
- Algorithms, data structures, cryptographic implementations, and security mechanisms
- Authentication and trust system designs
- User interface designs, wireframes, and visual assets
- Infrastructure configurations, deployment scripts, and DevOps tooling
- Business plans, product roadmaps, and feature specifications
- User data models and privacy architecture
- Any derivatives, notes, analyses, or summaries created from the above

Confidential Information does **not** include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient;
(b) Was already known to the Recipient prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records;
(c) Is independently developed by the Recipient without use of or reference to the Confidential Information; or
(d) Is lawfully obtained from a third party without restriction on disclosure.

## 3. Obligations of the Recipient

The Recipient agrees to:

1. **Not disclose** the Confidential Information to any third party without the Company's prior written consent.
2. **Not use** the Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Purpose stated in Section 1.
3. **Not copy, reproduce, or distribute** the source code, documentation, or any portion thereof, except as strictly necessary for the Purpose.
4. **Not reverse engineer, decompile, or create derivative works** based on the Confidential Information.
5. **Protect** the Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care used to protect their own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.
6. **Limit access** to the Confidential Information to those individuals who have a need to know for the Purpose and who are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those contained herein.
7. **Not upload, post, or transmit** any Confidential Information to public repositories, forums, social media, AI training services, or any publicly accessible location.
8. **Promptly notify** the Company of any unauthorized disclosure or use of the Confidential Information.

## 5. Return or Destruction of Materials

Upon the Company's written request, or upon termination of this Agreement, the Recipient shall:

1. Promptly return or destroy all copies of the Confidential Information in their possession, including electronic copies, local clones, backups, and printed materials.
2. Permanently delete the Confidential Information from all devices, cloud storage, and repositories.
3. Provide written certification of compliance within **ten (10) business days**.

## 6. Term and Termination

This Agreement is effective as of the date first written above and shall remain in force for a period of **two (2) years** from the date of disclosure, unless terminated earlier by either party with **thirty (30) days** written notice.

The confidentiality obligations in Sections 2, 3, and 4 shall survive termination of this Agreement and remain in effect for **five (5) years** from the date of disclosure, or for as long as the information qualifies as a trade secret under applicable law, whichever is longer.
